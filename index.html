About Me

Hello! My name is Federico but my friends call me Fed for short. Dating back to my adolescent days, I have always had a burning desire to learn how computers and the internet work. While I was devoted to the finance industry at the beginning of my professional career, I always had the end goal of pursuing my dream career in web development when the time was right.

Fortunately, that time arrived at the beginning of 2018 and I have been learning non-stop ever since. After extensive preparation, self-learning and research, I made the commitment to attend the 12-week full immersion accelerated web development program at LearningFuze (Irvine, CA) and graduated in September of 2018. I am beyond elated to start my dream career as a web developer and cannot wait to delve deeper into the vast world of computer programming.
